About us

Shree Kurumba Madhyamik Vidhyalaya(Kurumba MA VI) was established in 2012 B.S.
This school was established as a result of combined effort of Government of Nepal and the local people of Kurumba to provide quality of education for local people living in Kurumba.
Kurumba MA VI is the first school that keeps record of each student and teachers via computer software,and tracks their progress in each step.
